## Terms

### Adaptive Balanced Pull

A pull method that attempts to create varied voting options by considering factors such as genre, decade, runtime, and franchise.

---

### Event Series

A recurring schedule used to create Watch Party events.

Examples include weekly movie night, monthly special events, and television watch parties.

---

### Rotation

The current pool of eligible Watch Items available for voting.

---

### Watch Item

A movie, television series, or other media item managed by Watch Party Manager.

---

### Watch Item Journey

The complete history of a Watch Item, including suggestion, rotation appearances, votes, scheduled events, and watch records.

---

### Watch Party Backlot

The functional/admin channel where Watch Party Manager posts status, logs, schedules, and operational information.

---

### Watch Record

A completed viewing of a Watch Item.

A Watch Item may have multiple Watch Records if it is rewatched.
